Learn → Build → Submit → Get Reviewed → Earn Points → Get Certificate → Get Noticed → **Get Hired**

## 🤝 TEAMS COLLAB — The Game Changer

> **Apne dosto ke saath milke build karo. Saath mein jeeto.**

Yeh feature isliye add kar rahe hai kyunki agar log akele seekhte hai to akele hi chhod dete hai. But agar unka team ho — unke dost ho — to consistency aati hai. Accountability aati hai. Competition aati hai. Aur jitna zyada enjoy karoge utna zyada seekhoge.

### Kya hoga Teams Collab me:

- Koi bhi user **ek team bana sakta hai** (2–5 members tak MVP me)
- Team ka ek **unique naam aur avatar** hoga
- Team ke andar **projects assign honge**
- Har team member tasks complete karega
- **Individual points** bhi milenge + **team collective points** bhi milenge

### Team ke andar structure:

- **Team Leader** — Team create karta hai, members invite karta hai
- **Team Members** — Tasks complete karte hai, points contribute karte hai
- Team me **join request system** hoga (ya invite link)

---

## 🏆 LEADERBOARD SYSTEM — Do Types

### 1. 🧍 Solo Leaderboard

- Individual developers ki ranking
- Based on: tasks completed, points earned, streaks, contributions
- Time filters: **Weekly / Monthly / All-Time**

### 2. 👥 Teams Leaderboard

- Teams ki collective ranking
- Based on: combined team points, projects completed, tasks done
- Time filters: **Weekly / Monthly / All-Time**
- Team ka overall score = sum of all member contributions

**Why two leaderboards?**
Kyunki solo developer bhi shine kare aur teams bhi healthy competition feel kare. Dono worlds ka best.

---

## 🏛️ HALL OF FAME — Do Types

### 1. 🧍 Solo Hall of Fame

- Har month ka **top individual developer** permanently featured rehga
- Name, avatar, achievements, top project dikhai dega
- "Consistent Coder of the Month" badge milega
- Resume aur LinkedIn pe share ho sakta hai

### 2. 👥 Teams Hall of Fame

- Har month ki **top performing team** permanently featured rahegi
- Team name, members, projects, collective impact dikhai dega
- "Dream Team of the Month" badge milega
- Saare team members ko certificate milega

**Why both?**
Log apne naam ke liye bhi compete karte hai aur apne group ki reputation ke liye bhi. Dono motivations ko honor karna chahte hai hum.

# ☁️ 9. VERCEL FREE PLAN — CAN WE HANDLE DAILY USERS?

## What Vercel Free Plan Gives You (Hobby Tier):

| Resource | Free Limit |
|---|---|
| Bandwidth | 100 GB/month |
| Serverless Function Invocations | 100,000/month |
| Function Execution Time | 10 seconds max per request |
| Edge Requests | Unlimited (static) |
| Custom Domain | ✅ Supported |
| Build Minutes | 6,000 min/month |

## For a React SPA (your current setup):

Your frontend is a **static React build** — Vercel basically serves HTML/CSS/JS files from their global CDN.

- **Static files = essentially unlimited requests** — CDN handles it
- 100GB bandwidth is **massive** for early stage
- A typical page = ~500KB–2MB assets
- 100GB ÷ 1MB avg = **~100,000 page visits/month easily**
- That's **~3,300 users/day** before even hitting limits

## The Backend is the Real Question:

Your backend (Node.js APIs, MongoDB) will NOT be on Vercel. You'll need:

| Service | Free Tier |
|---|---|
| **Railway** | $5 free credit/month (good for ~500 hrs) |
| **Render** | 750 free hours/month (spins down after inactivity) |
| **MongoDB Atlas** | 512MB free cluster (plenty for early stage) |
| **Clerk** (Auth) | 10,000 monthly active users free |

## Honest Assessment:

| Scenario | Vercel Free? | Safe? |
|---|---|---|
| 0–500 daily users | ✅ Easily handles | ✅ No risk |
| 500–2,000 daily users | ✅ Handles (static CDN) | ⚠️ Watch backend |
| 2,000–5,000 daily users | ✅ Vercel fine | ❌ Backend will need upgrade |
| 5,000+ daily users | ✅ Vercel fine | ❌ Move to paid backend |

## Will the site break?

**The site itself (frontend) will NOT break on Vercel free plan.**
Vercel is battle-tested and the CDN is rock solid.

The risk is your **backend API** (Rate limits, cold starts on free Render, etc.)

## Strategy:

1. **Vercel** = Frontend (keep it here, it's free + excellent) ✅
2. **Railway / Render** = Backend Node.js (free to start, upgrade when users grow)
3. **MongoDB Atlas** = Database (free 512MB cluster, upgrade later)
4. **Clerk** = Authentication (10K MAU free — very generous)
5. When you hit scale → migrate backend to a $5–10/month VPS

## Bottom Line:

- **Vercel free plan is more than enough for 0 → 2,000 daily users**
- **Total monthly infra cost to start: $0**
- **Site will not break from Vercel's side**
- **You can handle real traffic before spending a single rupee**
